# Background On Me

## Who I Am

**Name:** Mike
**Handle:** Mikecranesync (GitHub)
**Role:** Solo founder and engineer of FactoryLM

30 years of industrial maintenance experience. Building FactoryLM to turn that domain expertise into a **maintenance copilot** that helps other maintenance technicians do their jobs better.

## What FactoryLM Is

An industrial IoT platform that connects to factory floor PLCs, collects operational data, and uses AI agents to assist maintenance technicians with diagnostics, quality assessment, and knowledge management. The goal: make a working maintenance copilot that a real technician can use.

## Technical Background

- **Primary language:** Python (Django, FastAPI, Celery)
- **Full-stack:** Python backend + JavaScript/TypeScript frontend
- **AI-assisted development:** Heavy use of Claude and AI pair programming to build and ship fast
- **Infrastructure:** PLCs, Raspberry Pi edge gateways, cloud services

## Current Stack

- **Code repo:** `Mikecranesync/factorylm` (monorepo at `~/factorylm/`)
